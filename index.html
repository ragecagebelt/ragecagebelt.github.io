About the NFL Title Belt

When Andrew Latham's West Washington Walruses won the 2016 Rage Cage Season, they started the belt. The next team they lost to in the '17 season took the belt from them, and the next team that team lost to took it, and so on. The belt will not be lost during ties, and will follow a team into the consolation ladder should they not qualify for the playoffs.

  • The Title Belt is active any week where an NFL game is played, regardless if the current Title Belt holder plays. This means the current Title Belt holder can keep the Title Belt during a playoff week when they are not playing . Bye weeks and weeks where the Title Belt holder is not qualified to play are indicated by a faded logo.
  • The following is used for tracking information
    • The team tracked as "Dory/Heath" was represented by just Dory Patterson prior to the 2019 Season
    • The team tracked as "Laura/John" was officially represented by just Laura Morgan prior to the 2018 Season
    • The teams tracked as "Sean Raffetto" and "Heath Hudgins" are no longer tracked in the league
